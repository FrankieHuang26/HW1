About

The restaurant started from chef Huang who realized how overpriced food has become and wanted to change the persepctive that comfort food is sometimes better. SAWA is that restaurant. Chef Huang wanted people to enjoy good comfort food that doesn't hurt their wallet.

Why SAWA? Sawa offers classic dishes such as burgers with french fries, classic cheese pizza, Margherita pizza, and out signature heros! or if you want something different then try our special menu which has japanese wagyu sandwich, asian inspired ceaser salad, and BBQ.

Eat like you're at home!.
